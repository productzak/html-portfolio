The Best Movies According to Zak

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Star Wars: A New Hope

Whiny Luke watches mentor he met a day before die and then exact revenge on his dad.

Star Wars: Empire Strikes Back

Dad comes home from the store to let son know he is his father.

Star Wars: Return of the Jedi

Dad feels bad for cutting son's hand off, so he decides to go to anger management classes, but it is too late.
